Wind

Access data and analysis services that provide access to wind resource data and NREL models.

Education and Training Programs (/api/windexchange/educationprojects)

Access locations of wind energy education and training programs in the United States at community colleges, universities, and other institutions.

School Wind Project Locations (/api/windexchange/schoolprojects)

Access locations of installed and planned school wind energy projects in the United States for K-12, community colleges, universities, and other education centers.

Wind Toolkit Data (/api/wind/wind_toolkit_extract)

Collect and download, as CSV, a configurable set of data fields from a national collection of wind stations.
